Test instance architecture (as built and run)

This is the prototype as tested against synthetic data - what ran to produce the measured results. Each message is classified, routed, and drafted into a reply for human review. Every message ends at a human before anything sends. Below the main flow, a feedback loop tags real outcomes and feeds account health/VoC signals back in to soften confidence and shape draft tone on future messages. Dashed boxes are mock stand-ins for real systems here.
Fixed logic, no AI call AI model call (fixed step) AI model call (agent decides, conditional) Safety gate - always required External system (dashed = mock/illustrative in this prototype) Feedback-loop signal source (mock)
Safety overrides (sensitive topics, retention risk) win at "score + route" regardless of confidence. Sensitive topics and retention risk are never auto-resolved. The feedback loop only ever nudges confidence and tone (health_risk_confidence_penalty in config.py) - it never overrides a routing decision, and the calibration report only surfaces patterns for a human to act on.
